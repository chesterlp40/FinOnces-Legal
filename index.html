Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

FinOnces respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the FinOnces mobile application.

Information We Collect

FinOnces does not collect, store, or transmit personal data to external servers.

All financial information you enter in the app, including transactions, payment methods and financial reports, is stored locally on your device.

FinOnces does not require account creation and does not track users.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally using Apple system frameworks. If you enable device backups or cloud synchronization provided by Apple, your data may be included in those backups according to Apple’s policies.

Subscriptions

FinOnces offers optional in-app subscriptions to unlock premium features. Payments and subscription management are handled directly by Apple through the App Store.

Advertising

The free version of FinOnces may display advertisements provided by third-party advertising networks.

Data Security

Because your financial data remains on your device, you are responsible for protecting your device using passcode, Face ID, or Touch ID.

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact FinOnces Support at support@finonces.app.
